Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · VirtualFit Pro

Photo & Image Privacy

Your photos are NEVER stored on our servers. When you use our Virtual Try-On feature, your photos are processed in real-time and immediately deleted.

Here's exactly what happens when you upload a photo:

  • Your photo is compressed in your browser before sending
  • Sent securely over HTTPS encryption to our server
  • Forwarded to Fashn.ai for AI processing only
  • The AI generates your try-on result and returns it
  • Your original photo is immediately deleted — not saved anywhere
  • The result image is hosted temporarily by Fashn.ai (auto-expires in 24 hours)

Fashn.ai processes your image under their own privacy policy: fashn.ai/privacy

Data We Collect

When you create an account and shop with us, we collect:

  • Name & Email — for your account and order confirmations
  • Phone number — for delivery updates (optional)
  • Delivery address — only when you place an order
  • Order history — so you can track your purchases
  • Try-on history — which products you tried on (product IDs only, not photos)

We never collect: your photos, payment card details, or sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Data

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmation and tracking updates
  • Improve our product recommendations
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails (only if you opt-in)

We never sell your data to third parties. We never use your data for advertising outside our platform.

How We Protect Your Data

  •  All data transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  •  Passwords are hashed — we cannot see your password
  •  Database stored on Supabase with enterprise security
  •  API keys stored in environment variables, not in code
  •  Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  •  Admin panel password protected and separate from customer website

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete — request deletion of all your data
  • Correct — update incorrect information
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime

Delete My Data

Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data. This cannot be undone.
